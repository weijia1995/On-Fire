What it is:
A duo of buttery soft and moisturizing lip-balm-meets-lipstick hybrids.

What it is formulated to do:
 Lip Whip moisturizes for hours without feeling heavy, oily, or sticky. Each is all natural and features eco-luxe organic ingredients and premium glass packaging. With its bright, opaque pigment, Jolene makes a statement worn alone or pairs with Shimmer for a bold rosé champagne shine. Shimmer is the perfect sparkling nude champagne that plays well with your favorite Lip Whips or your favorite lipstick.

This set contains:
- 0.25 oz Color Lip Whip in Shimmer
- 0.25 oz Color Lip Whip in Jolene

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:
 These products are also gluten-free, cruelty-free, and petroleum-free. Each is hand made in Seattle, WA. They can also be used to tame flyaways or as cuticle moisturizers.What it is:
