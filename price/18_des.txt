What it is:
 An all-in-one, rinse-free cleansing water for all skin types.

What it does:
 This oil-free, lathering Call Time Gentle Cleansing Water gently removes makeup and impurities from the face, eyes, and lips. Its calming formula gently cleans and rejuvenates tired skin, while hydrating and renewing, leaving even the most sensitive skin with a soft healthy-looking glow. 

What it is formulated WITHOUT:
- Parabens
- Sulfates
- Phthalates

What else you need to know:
 This product is vegan, free of gluten, made with certified organic ingredients, and recommended for all skin types. All Cinema Secrets PRO Cosmetics are cruelty-free.What it is:
