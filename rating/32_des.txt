What it is:
A luxurious body moisturizer with the exotic scent of Oud Wood.

What it does:
This rich cream nourishes and moisturizes skin and leaves it hydrated and smooth. Oud wood is one of the most rare, precious, and expensive ingredients in a perfumer’s arsenal. Exotic rosewood and cardamom give way to a smoky blend of rare oud wood, sandalwood, and vetiver. Tonka bean and amber add warmth and sensuality.

Notes:
Rare Oud Wood, Sandalwood, Chinese Pepper, Rosewood, Tonka Bean, Vanilla.
