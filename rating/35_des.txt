What it is:
A multifunctional oil with a relaxing scent that can be used on the face, body, hair, and nails.

What it is formulated to do:
This rich, multi-benefit treatment oil to adds a soft sheen and provides intensive nourishing benefits while plumping and softening the skin. It can be mixed with cream, used under foundation, or added to the bath as well as used directly to benefit hair, face, and body. The oil features a luxurious scent and melts into the skin instantly without a greasy feel. 

What else you need to know:
