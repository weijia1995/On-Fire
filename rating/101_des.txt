What it is:A medium-hold, fast-drying, non-aerosol spray for hair that creates definition and lasting, touchable style.
What it is formulated to do:Made with organic ingredients, Rahua Defining Hair Spray creates definition, hold, and lasting, touchable style while offering UV protection. What it is formulated WITHOUT:- Parabens- Sulfates - Phthalates
