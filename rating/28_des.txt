What it is:
A TOM FORD, matte lipstick that imparts a powerfully sophisticated look of unadulterated, full-coverage color.

What it does:
 Formulated with a luxurious, velvet matte finish, the lipstick smooths comfortably onto the lips. It features a spectrum of richly pigmented huesfrom sensuous pink neutrals to alluring bolds.
