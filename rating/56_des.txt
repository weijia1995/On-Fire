What it is:
A high pigment black mascara with a volumizing and curling effect.

What it does:
 This mascara brings high volume to every single lash, and its creamy formula sweeps on intense, black pigments that keep lashes clear and rich all day. Smudge-proof and easily rinsed away with warm water, it’s enriched with the Black Jem Complex to nourish your lashes. The brush is designed by Creative Director Chosungah in a bullet shape to allow for stunningly voluminous lashes.What it is:
